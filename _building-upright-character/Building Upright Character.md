<h2>Building Upright Character</h2>
<p>A spectrum of customized modular programmes complement classroom learning.
We want to provide a rich learning experience for students to participate
in modular activities in the arts, sports and games so as to facilitate
their all-round development and for some students, to discover their talents
and extend their interests.</p>
