<h2>CCA</h2>
<p>CCA is conducted on Friday mornings for students from P4 to P6. Additional
CCA training may be conducted on selected Monday, Wednesday or Thursday
afternoons for students representing the school in competitions or performances.&nbsp;
It includes Sports &amp; Games, Visual &amp; Performing Arts, Clubs &amp;
Societies and Uniformed Group. CCA provides learning opportunities for
students to deepen their passion and talents.</p>
