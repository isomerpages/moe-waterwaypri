<h2>Parents Support Group</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Parents/psg_purpose.png">
</div>
<h4>Our Vision</h4>
<p>A Parent Support Group based on trust, passion and professionalism, guided
by the school’s values of Respect, Integrity, Self-discipline and Empathy,
for the well-being of the children in Waterway Primary School.</p>
<h4>Our Mission</h4>
<p>• Be supportive partners in education.
<br>• Work in partnership with WAPS to develop a supportive parent community
and encourage positive partnerships for our children’s education.
<br>• Reach out to other parents to contribute towards developing a rich and
vibrant learning environment in WAPS.</p>
<h2>Join Us!</h2>
